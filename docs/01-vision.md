# GymOS Vision

## Product
GymOS - Gym Management + Member Experience Platform

## Customers
- Gym Owners (paying customers)
- Trainers
- Gym Members

## Core Features
- Gym Discovery
- Equipment Management
- Membership Management
- QR Check-in
- Workout Plans
- Trainer Assistance Requests
- Analytics Dashboard
- Multi-language Support

## Revenue Model
Monthly subscription from gym owners.
